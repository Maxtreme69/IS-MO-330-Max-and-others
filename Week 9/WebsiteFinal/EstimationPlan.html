Estimation Plan
MVF 1
The first MVF is creating the project board within the scrum board. This feature will allow users to create and setup the overall board. The research for this feature could take up to 8 hours and the design part of the feature will take up to 3 hours. Lastly, coding is estimated to take 8 hours and testing should take 1 hour. Additionally, this feature also relies on implementing a database which can take additional time and effort to implement. This could take an additional 5-10 hours for research and implementation if assistance is required due to the difficulty of the MVF. We’ve estimated the ‘manage project’ feature to take 21 hours to complete.
MVF 2
The ‘manage sticky notes’ MVF feature contains the ability to add, delete and edit each of the sticky notes. This feature is one of the simpler ones but can take time to implement properly. This feature could require up to 4 hours of research, 2 hours in design, 7 hours of coding and 1 hour of testing.
MVF 3
The drag and drop feature enables the ability to move sticky notes to different columns within the scrum board. This feature is essential within a scrum board as it allows the management of sticky notes. It can take up to 3 hours of research time, 1 hour to design, 4 hours to code and testing could be done within an 1 hour. The total amount of time for this MVF feature is estimated to take 9 hours.
MVF 4
The ‘calendar’ feature mainly consists of presenting a calendar and showing any reminders or projects that will be due, implementing this means connecting the due dates and reminders within the calendar feature. The research time for this feature is expected to be 4 hours, design should take 1 hour, coding could take up to 5 hours and testing can be done in less than 1 hour, leading the final total to be 11 hours.
MVF 5
The last MVF ‘progress tracking feature’ will allow users to track the state of the scrum board throughout different dates of the project, although this feature sounds very simple in concept most of the group consider this the hardest feature as it contains a carousel wheel which is used for the dates, the save states will also be implemented through a database, which we’ve already explored using SQL databases in phpMyAdmin, since most of team members lack a lot of knowledge in this technology, this is one of the harder features. The research period for this feature is expected to be around 10 hours, design should be done within hours, coding could take up to 16 hours and testing could be done in 2 hours, making a total of 31 hours.
EVF 1
Uploading user photo, this EVF should be a fairly simple feature as it requires an image to be uploaded in the profile picture section, this feature should take a relatively short amount of time to implement, research could take 1 hour, design 1 hour, coding less than an hour and testing could take 30 mins.
EVF 2
Task Allocation, this EVF is relatively simple as it requires users placing the nametag of users on given tasks (on sticky notes), research should take 2 hours, design 1 hour and coding could take 1-2 hours, testing could be done in approximately 30 mins.
EVF 3
Layout customizability, this feature should allow customizability of sticky notes and layout colours, text size and font. This feature could take a longer period of time due to the amount of customizability options. This features research could take up to 5-10 hours, design could take 2 hours, coding 5-10 hours and testing should take 1 hour.
